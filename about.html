About TradeStone

At TradeStone, we’re aiming to build the Globes most trusted platform for tradespeople, contractors, and DIYers. Our mission is simple: to connect skilled professionals, resourceful individuals, and top-quality materials—all in one place.

TradeStone is more than a marketplace. We’re a complete business ecosystem for the modern tradesperson:

  • Buy and sell surplus materials with ease
  • Promote your services and get discovered by local customers
  • Apply for private and government contract opportunities
  • Use our built-in invoicing and escrow systems to manage payments professionally
  • Get support from our AI assistant for technical queries, pricing, and planning
  • Join a forum of like-minded experts and DIYers for honest advice and collaboration

Whether you're a contractor running a crew or a homeowner tackling a new project, TradeStone gives you the tools to work smarter, find trusted help, and save time and money.

We're proudly independent, mobile-first, and committed to transparency, fairness, and empowering the trades. Every feature we build supports one goal: to help tradespeople succeed.

Welcome to TradeStone—where tools, talent, and trust come together.
